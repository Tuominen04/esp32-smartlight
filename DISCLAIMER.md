- **Voltage Compatibility**: Verify voltage ratings before installation (typically 3.3V/5V DC)

- **Licensed Electrician**: For AC mains integration, consult a licensed electrician

- **WiFi Security**: Use WPA2/WPA3 encryption for WiFi connections
